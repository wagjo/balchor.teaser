Did you know that household chores are a great way to build a lasting sense of mastery, responsibility and self-reliance? According to the Wall Street Journal, young adults who began chores at their early age were more likely to have good relationships, to achieve early career success and to be self-sufficient.

However, today’s hectic world makes it especially hard to schedule and assign household chores in a way that is responsive to children’s needs and is respectful of their individual abilities, talents and personalities. Figuring out the right amount of responsibility is a balancing act.

That’s why we are creating a unique tool for mothers that strive to raise their kids as best as they can. Our Balanced Chores application will help you to prepare, schedule and evaluate your household chores in a convenient way with little effort. This invaluable tool will empower you to:

  • Save time that is needed to prepare household chores charts without sacrificing their value for your family, by automating the planning, scheduling and creation of chores
  • Receive weekly chores charts specifically tailored to the needs of your family, reflecting your children’s talents and personalities
  • Discover which chores suits your loved ones the best, and let them spend their time on things that build relationships and bring them success and happiness
  • Teach your children to be kind, helpful and responsive to others’ needs, by assigning them chores that are best suited for their age, skills and personality
  • Watch how your kids are progressing and understand them better, with comprehensive yet easy to understand chores reports
  • Build the sense of accomplishment with fun and loving badges, appreciate your kids’ skills and achievements with certificates and awards
